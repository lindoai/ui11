Usage Guidelines

  • • Use Key Metrics for dashboard displays and business reporting
  • • Achievement Stats work well for about pages and company milestones
  • • Counter Stats are perfect for landing pages and marketing materials
  • • Comparison Stats effectively showcase improvements and case studies
  • • Ensure icons and colors match your brand design system
  • • Keep numbers realistic and update them regularly for credibility

Accessibility Features

  • • High contrast ratios ensure readability in all themes
  • • Large font sizes make numbers easily readable
  • • Color coding is supplemented with icons and text
  • • Proper semantic markup for screen reader compatibility
  • • Keyboard navigation support for interactive elements
  • • Focus indicators visible on all interactive components
